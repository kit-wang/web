polyglot


[paa·lee·glaat] noun
A person who enjoys learning and using several languages.

Learning a language is not only studying its lexicon. It is entrenching oneself in a different culture and growing from those vibrant experiences. Each language is uniquely distinct, and full of its own rich history. Explore and learn about each of the four selected languages through this website!
